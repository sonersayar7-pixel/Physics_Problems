## Section 9: Momentum Comparison

**Formula:** $p = m \cdot v$

| Object | Mass ($m$) | Velocity ($v$) | Momentum ($p$) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fly** | $0.002 \text{ kg}$ | $10 \text{ m/s}$ | $0.02 \text{ kg}\cdot\text{m/s}$ |
| **Tennis Ball** | $0.060 \text{ kg}$ | $1 \text{ m/s}$ | $0.06 \text{ kg}\cdot\text{m/s}$ |

**Conclusion:** The tennis ball has **3 times** the momentum of the fly ($0.06$ vs $0.02 \text{ kg}\cdot\text{m/s}$).
