## Section 1: Projectile Motion Analysis

**Parameters:** $v_0 = 100 \text{ m/s}$, $\theta = 37^\circ$, $g = 9.8 \text{ m/s}^2$

### Equations of Motion
* **Acceleration:** $a_x = 0$, $a_y = -g$
* **Velocity:** $v_x = v_0 \cos\theta$, $v_y = v_0 \sin\theta - gt$
* **Position:** $x(t) = (v_0 \cos\theta)t$, $y(t) = (v_0 \sin\theta)t - \frac{1}{2}gt^2$

### Summary of Results
| Quantity | Formula | Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Time of Flight** | $t_f = \frac{2v_0 \sin\theta}{g}$ | $\approx 12.24 \text{ s}$ |
| **Max Height** | $H = \frac{(v_0 \sin\theta)^2}{2g}$ | $\approx 183.67 \text{ m}$ |
| **Range** | $R = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}$ | $\approx 979.2 \text{ m}$ |
