## Section 5: Relative Velocity Analysis

**Parameters:**
* River Velocity ($v_r$): $2 \text{ m/s}$ [East]
* Boat Speed ($v_b$): $5 \text{ m/s}$
* Distance ($d$): $200 \text{ m}$ [North]

### Navigation Equations
To maintain a Northward heading, the steering angle $\theta$ is:
$$\theta = \arcsin\left(\frac{v_{river}}{v_{boat}}\right) = 23.58^\circ \text{ West of North}$$

The resultant velocity magnitude $v_N$ is:
$$v_N = \sqrt{v_{boat}^2 - v_{river}^2} = \sqrt{25 - 4} = \sqrt{21} \approx 4.58 \text{ m/s}$$
